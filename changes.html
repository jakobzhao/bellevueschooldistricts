Boundary Change

From closed schools to receiving schools: how boundary change happened

This page reconstructs the relationship before and after Bellevue's 2023 elementary consolidation using public reporting and Bellevue School District planning materials. The current attendance-area polygons are available; the historical Wilburton and Eastgate polygons still require archived GIS data or vectorization from older maps.

本页根据公开报道和 Bellevue School District 的规划材料，重建 2023 年小学整合前后的变化关系。当前入学区域 polygon 已经可用；旧版 Wilburton 与 Eastgate 的精确边界仍需要历史 GIS 或旧地图矢量化。

这些流向不是几何边界本身，而是政策变化产生的学生与项目迁移关系，帮助确定哪些当前边界值得重点检查。

What To Compare / 比较什么

Read the change page together with the map

变化页应该和地图页一起读。

Geometry change / 几何变化

If historical attendance boundaries become available, compare old Wilburton/Eastgate areas with current Clyde Hill, Enatai, Spiritridge, and Woodridge areas by area, perimeter, compactness, and convexity.

如果能取得旧版入学边界，可以把旧 Wilburton/Eastgate 区域与当前 Clyde Hill、Enatai、Spiritridge、Woodridge 区域叠加，比较面积、周长、紧凑度和凸性。

Student flow / 学生流向

Even without old polygons, the reassignment flow is an analytical object: which schools closed, which schools received students, which programs moved, and which families had to adapt.

即使没有旧 polygon，也可以先分析 reassignment flow：哪些学校关闭，哪些学校接收，哪些项目迁移，哪些家庭需要适应新的学校路径。

Equity question / 公平性质疑

The key question is not whether boundaries became more compact, but whether the change altered travel burden, program access, peer networks, language support, and family costs.

真正关键的问题不是边界是否变得更紧凑，而是变化是否改变了通勤距离、项目可达性、同伴网络、多语言支持和家庭负担。

Equity Lens / 公平视角

Change Impact Matrix / 变化影响矩阵

Travel burden / 通勤负担

Did distance, time, or transportation become harder after reassignment?

重新分配后，距离、时间或交通安排是否变得更困难？

Program access / 项目可达性

Did advanced learning, special education, or after-school access become harder for some students?

高级学习、特殊教育或课后服务是否对某些学生更难获得？

Community continuity / 社区连续性

Were students separated from peer networks, teachers, or neighborhood school identity?

学生是否被拆离同伴网络、老师关系或社区学校身份？

Income and housing / 收入与住房

Were renter, lower-income, or lower-housing-cost areas asked to absorb more disruption?

租房、低收入或房价较低区域是否承担了更多变化成本？

Language and participation / 语言与参与

Did multilingual families receive enough notice, translation, meeting access, and response channels?

多语言家庭是否获得足够通知、翻译、会议机会和反馈渠道？

Resources following students / 资源是否跟随学生

Did receiving schools get enough teachers, classrooms, counseling, transportation, and specialized services?

接收学校是否获得足够教师、教室、辅导、交通和特殊服务资源？

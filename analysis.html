Analysis

Question boundaries with math, and question math itself

This project does not claim that Bellevue's school boundaries are "gerrymandered." It tests how far mathematical indicators can go in local boundary questions. Compactness, convex hull ratio, and minimum rectangle ratio make boundary shape measurable, but they cannot by themselves explain school closure, reassignment, travel burden, program relocation, housing inequality, or public participation.

这个项目不是要证明 Bellevue 学区边界被“操纵”，而是测试数学指标在地方边界问题中能走多远。紧凑度、凸包比例、最小矩形比例可以把边界形状变成可比较的证据，但它们不能单独解释学校关闭、学生重新分配、通勤负担、项目迁移、住房差异或公共参与。

1

What math can do

Metrics can quickly flag boundaries with unusual shapes. Bennett, Newport Heights, Clyde Hill, and Spiritridge are useful examples because they move the discussion from "this looks odd" to "which boundaries are geometrically unusual within the same scale?"

数学指标可以快速标记形状异常的边界。例如 Bennett、Newport Heights、Clyde Hill 和 Spiritridge 能把讨论从“感觉边界奇怪”转为“哪些边界在同一尺度下显著不规则”。

2

What math cannot prove

Low compactness does not directly prove unfairness. A boundary may be irregular because of lakes, roads, hills, capacity balancing, school locations, or administrative habit. A visually regular boundary can still create unequal impacts.

低紧凑度不能直接证明不公平。边界可能因为湖泊、道路、山坡、容量平衡、校舍位置或行政习惯而不规则；反过来，规则边界也可能造成不均等影响。

3

Why Bellevue is a useful test

Bellevue has measurable boundary data and a real policy context. The 2023 Wilburton and Eastgate consolidation shifts the question from "is the shape compact?" to "did boundary and school decisions distribute impacts unevenly?"

Bellevue 同时有可测量的边界数据和真实政策语境。2023 年 Wilburton 与 Eastgate 的整合，使问题从“形状是否紧凑”转为“边界和学校决策是否把影响不均等地分配给不同家庭”。

Social Value

A tool for public discussion before boundary decisions

Explore mathematical edge effects

When a school district considers consolidation or boundary adjustment, this tool gives families, researchers, and decision makers a way to first ask whether the proposed or existing boundary produces unusual geometric effects at the edges.

当学区考虑合并或调整边界时，这个工具至少提供了一个探索入口：让家庭、研究者和决策者先看到现有或拟议边界是否在数学上产生了一些边缘效应。

Connect metrics to consequences

The point is not to let a compactness score make the decision. The point is to use the metric as an early warning system that asks what may be affected next: elementary student travel, program access, school capacity, neighborhood continuity, and unequal burdens across communities.

重点不是让紧凑度分数替我们做决定，而是把指标作为早期提醒：它提示我们继续追问，哪些东西可能被影响，比如小学生通勤、项目可达性、学校容量、社区连续性，以及不同社区承担的负担是否不平等。

Make equity questions visible earlier

This gives the research social value: it turns mathematical analysis into a shared foundation for discussion, so equity and justice questions can appear before, not only after, boundary changes are implemented.

这正是这个研究的社会价值：它把数学分析转化为一个共同讨论的基础，让公平正义问题能够在边界变化实施之前就被看见，而不是等影响发生之后才被讨论。

Methodological Critique

How to evaluate and question the math

Do not treat the metric as the judge

Polsby-Popper is very sensitive to perimeter. A winding boundary lowers the score, but winding may come from natural geography or neighborhood edges rather than unfair design.

Polsby-Popper 对周长非常敏感。边界越曲折，分数越低，但曲折可能来自自然地理或街区边缘，不一定来自不公平设计。

Compare several metrics

Convex hull ratio and minimum rectangle ratio supplement Polsby-Popper. If several measures are low, the geometry deserves attention; if only one measure is low, the interpretation should be cautious.

凸包比例和最小矩形比例可以补充 Polsby-Popper。如果多个指标都低，几何异常更值得关注；如果只有一个指标低，就需要谨慎解释。

Put math back into school context

In Bellevue, the point is not to find the "ugliest" boundary. The stronger question is whether low-compactness boundaries intersect with consolidation, receiving schools, program movement, travel distance, and family burden.

对 Bellevue 来说，重点不是找“最丑”的边界，而是看低紧凑度边界是否与学校整合、接收学校、项目迁移、交通距离和家庭负担相交叉。

News Context

Public discussion and criticism

News coverage does not replace data analysis, but it identifies which questions the math should help investigate.

新闻报道不能替代数据分析，但它能告诉我们应该用数学指标去追问哪些问题。

Process speed and participation

KUOW and KOMO reported parent criticism around fast timelines, limited early engagement, confusing communication, and concerns that consolidation moved too quickly.

KUOW 和 KOMO 报道了家长对关闭时间线过快、早期参与不足、沟通混乱和推进过快的批评。

Where students were reassigned

Public reporting describes Wilburton students moving toward Clyde Hill and Enatai, Eastgate students toward Spiritridge, and the Spiritridge advanced learning program toward Woodridge.

公开报道显示，Wilburton 学生转向 Clyde Hill 与 Enatai，Eastgate 学生转向 Spiritridge，Spiritridge 的高级学习项目迁往 Woodridge。

Equity claims and district response

Northwest Asian Weekly reported criticism that closures could displace lower-income and diverse student communities, while the district emphasized budget shortfalls, enrollment decline, and the need to rightsize services.

Northwest Asian Weekly 报道了关于低收入和多元学生群体被迁出的批评；学区则强调预算短缺、入学人数下降和维持服务所需的 rightsizing。

Social Justice Layer

Adding equity and justice to measurement

If the analysis stops at compactness, it remains a shape analysis. The equity layer asks who gains access, who absorbs costs, and who participates in decisions.

如果分析停留在紧凑度，它仍然只是形状分析。公平正义层要求我们追问：谁获得机会，谁承担成本，谁参与决策。

Burden distribution

Check whether distance, travel time, transportation, and family pickup costs shifted disproportionately to some communities.

检查距离、通勤时间、交通方式和家庭接送成本是否被不成比例地转移到某些社区。

Opportunity access

Track whether advanced learning, special education, language support, and after-school care became easier or harder to access.

追踪高级学习、特殊教育、语言支持和课后照护等项目是否变得更容易或更难获得。

Structural inequality

Overlay boundary changes with income, housing cost, renter share, race, language, and transportation access.

将边界变化与收入、住房成本、租房比例、族裔、语言和交通可达性叠加。

Procedural justice

Evaluate whether families had time, information, translation, meeting access, and meaningful response channels.

评价家庭是否有足够时间、信息、翻译、会议渠道和真实反馈机会。
